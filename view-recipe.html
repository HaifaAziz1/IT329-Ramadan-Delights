Sambosa

Creator

Sara Ahmed

Category

Ramadan Iftar

Description

Crispy sambosa filled with cheese and herbs — perfect for iftar.

Ingredients

  • 10 sambosa wrappers
  • 1 cup cheese
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix cheese with parsley.
  2. Fill wrappers and seal edges.
  3. Fry until golden.
  4. Serve hot.
